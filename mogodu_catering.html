About the Mobile Kitchen

After many years of planing and saving, Mogodu Monday hit the road, literally, with the purchase of a party-on-wheels truck with a custom-built sound system, plasma screen and restaurant-grade kitchen that provides street food such as smash burgers, New York-style hot dogs and gourmet ‘boerie’ rolls. After a thorough DIY fittings (trust us!) the truck was retrofitted into a classic commercial kitchen with state-of-the art equipment.

The Mobile kitchen allows us to accommodate all demands, ranging from large corporate catering events to private dining parties. Just save us a parking space.

A mobile restaurant is a great alternative for catering your wedding, event, or party. We do more than just deliver, we prepare your food from fresh ingredients and deliver it hot. Mogodu Monday is ideal for those venues that lack an on-site food concession.
